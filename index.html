Book Report: "Palace of Illusions" by Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni

“Palace of Illusion” retells the story of the epic Mahabharata from Draupadi’s perspective. Born from a fire, Draupadi navigates through a world dominated by men, her life intertwined with the Pandavas, her husbands and their rivals, the Kauravas. The story explores the themes of feminism, destiny and complex relationship, particularly with Karna and her bond with Krishna. Divakaruni’s writing brings ancient India to life, through Paanchali’s perspective, highlighting her inner struggles and strength. This novel offers readers with a fresh, feminist take on a classic epic. I would recommend this novel to anyone who is interested in ancient mythology, historical fiction and adventure.
